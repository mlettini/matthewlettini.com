Work

I’ve spent the last 11 years of my career improving Harvest. In that time I’ve worked on just about everything—the core functionality, all new products and apps, big feature flows, tons of small UX iterations, the design system, and even swag… too much to list in a portfolio. So here’s just a brief selection of highlights I’m proud to have helped bring to life during my tenure:

    Simple, flexible, and visual time planning for teams.

  2. Time Tracking Reminders

    Prompts to help customers build a habit with time keeping.

  3. Welcome Flow

    Greeting new trials and setting them up for success.

  4. Cancellation Flow

    Providing a pleasant and useful close account experience.

  5. Harvest for Mac v1

    Hotkey time tracking right from the desktop.

  6. Harvest for iPhone v2

    Simple on-the-go time and expense tracking.

  7. GetHarvest.com

    Multiple redesigns of Harvest’s marketing website.

  8. Chosen

    Javascript plugin to make long select boxes more user-friendly.

  9. Harvest Swag

    Keeping it fun in the family.
